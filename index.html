Balancing school and coding, I've consistently invested time into improving both my technical depth and creative vision — learning new languages, building real projects, refining my design sense, and thinking like a developer who solves problems, not just writes code. From 2024 to now, I've transformed curiosity into capability. And this is only the foundation of what I'm building next.

Mid 2024

Going Beyond the Frontend

I didn't stop at just frontend basics. I expanded into modern development by exploring React and building structured, component-based applications. Along the way, I learned programming fundamentals through Python and Java — which strengthened my problem-solving skills and helped me think beyond just designing interfaces. Each language gave a different perspective — Python's simplicity, Java's structured approach, JavaScript's flexibility.

That initial spark hasn't left since.
